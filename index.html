Frequently asked questions

Does Clean Copy rewrite or paraphrase my text?

No. Clean Copy does not change wording, phrasing, or meaning. It only removes hidden characters, formatting artifacts, and problematic symbols before paste.

Does Clean Copy use AI or send my text anywhere?

No. Clean Copy does not use AI and does not send or store your text. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Why isn’t Clean Copy on the Chrome Web Store yet?

Clean Copy is currently in early access while final review and listing preparations are underway.

Will this work on all websites?

Clean Copy works wherever standard copy and paste is supported in the browser. Some rich text editors may behave differently depending on the browser.

What happens after early access ends?

You’ll continue using Clean Copy normally, and future updates will be delivered through the Chrome Web Store once listed.
